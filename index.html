Wondering where to donate old books or textbooks?
You may donate the books and drop them off at our warehouse in Toronto or to your local charity.

Save trees

End deforestation and planted with www.trees.org. Trees for the Future fights problem with the Forest Garden Program, a process of planting specific types of fast-growing trees.

Education

Support Classrooms with www.donorschoose.org. DonorsChoose empowers public school teachers from across the country to request much-needed materials for their students.

Charity

Sending Books for Africa with www.betterworldbooks.com. We send most of your donated books to Kenya and Nigeria through various partners such as Better World Books.

How it works?

  • You donate 1 box of books = we plant 10 trees
  • You donate 1 box of books = we will fund a classroom project

If you live in another city and have a buyback order, you may include up to 10 lbs of donated books in your current buyback order in the same box. However, if you don’t have a buyback order, then we don’t pay for shipping for donated books at this time.

Our Mission by 2022

Our main goal is to save and plant as many trees as we can and also provide each customer with the opportunity to turn their unwanted textbooks into profit.
Donating books allows a chance for a second life, giving someone else the opportunity to read and enjoy the book. It’s also an environmentally-conscious decision since recycling prevents them from ending up in a landfill.
